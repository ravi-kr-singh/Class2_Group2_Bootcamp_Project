Visa Budit is a one stop application to budget your daily spending and plan how you want to spend your money. It provides seamless and cashless experience to shop wherever you want, whenever you want. Visa Budit provides you astonishing offers based on your nobility score. The more you donate, more offers you get and more you save. The donations made by Visa Budit are sent to well recognized Non-government organizations.

The home page displays the functionality of our website in an inviting manner. It lets you navigate to the registration page. Visa Budit application ensures that your card details are not stored in the application, with the help of Visa Token Service, your card details are in safe hands!

It performs Authorization and Authentication by sending you OTP to your registered mobile number and you can easily set up a password. What is better than a seamless, innovative experience with the best security measures. Your data is safe with us.

The application provides a variety of merchants and offers. You can select any merchant from the list to carry out a transaction, every time you carry out a transaction you will be notified if you want to donate a small amount to the list of our well recognized NGO’s. So, This means you can donate for good while you are shopping! Not, just this every time you donate to an NGO you will be given Nobility points on the basis of your donation. These Nobility points can be further used to redeem unbelievable offers on the products you love.

You can budget, donate, spend and save all using one single app – Visa Budit!
